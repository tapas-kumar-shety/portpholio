About Me.

MANHWA AND MANGA WRITER

I'm Kirito kirigaya a passionate manga and manwha writer inspired by the rich storytelling of classics like 'Berserk' and beloved anime such as 'One Piece,' as well as poignant romances like 'Golden Time' and 'Your Lie in April.' These experiences ignited my creative spark, driving me to become a writer dedicated to crafting captivating stories. Drawing from a diverse range of influences, I blend intricate world-building, compelling characters, and dynamic visuals to create immersive narratives that span genres. Whether exploring epic fantasy realms or heartfelt slice-of-life dramas, I aim to transport readers into unforgettable worlds filled with adventure, emotion, and suspense."
